Detailed Description

A SurfaceGL is the implementatin of PainterBackend::Surface for the GL backend. A SurfaceGL must only be used with at most one GL context (even GL contexts in the same share group cannot shader SurfaceGL objects).

explicit

Ctor. Use the passed GL texture to which to render content; the gl_texture must have as its texture target GL_TEXTURE_2D and must already have its backing store allocated (i.e. glTexImage or glTexStorage has been called on the texture). The texture object's ownership is NOT passed to the SurfaceGL, the caller is still responible to delete the texture (with GL) and the texture must not be deleted (or have its backing store changed via glTexImage) until the SurfaceGL is deleted. The viewport() is initialized to be exactly the entire backing store.
